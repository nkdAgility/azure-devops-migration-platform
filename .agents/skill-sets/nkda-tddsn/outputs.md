```text
  01-assessment.md
  02-target-test-suite.md
  03-architecture-update.md
  04-rebuild-plan.md
  05-implementation-summary.md
  06-verification.md
```

## Required Files

- `01-assessment.md`
- `02-target-test-suite.md`
- `03-architecture-update.md`
- `04-rebuild-plan.md`
- `05-implementation-summary.md`
- `06-verification.md`

No autonomous workflow is complete unless all six artefacts exist or the final report explicitly explains why one or more artefacts could not be produced.
